Iron Pot Millionaire

Iron Pot Millionaire is a ruthless, disciplined approach to building wealth by stripping life down to what actually matters.

  1. Define your “iron pot” baseline — the absolute minimum version of your lifestyle.
  2. Eliminate all non-essential spending for a defined season.
  3. Channel every available dollar into debt destruction and asset acquisition.
  4. Use clear milestones and timeframes to maintain intensity without burning out.

Wealth Architecture

Wealth Architecture is about designing your financial life like a system, not a series of random decisions.

  • Cash layer: emergency fund, short-term reserves.
  • Defense layer: insurance, buffers, risk management.
  • Offense layer: investments, income expansion, asymmetric bets.

Soon: Visual diagram and downloadable Wealth Architecture PDF.

Scenario Planning

Scenario Planning gives you predefined playbooks for when life hits — so you’re not improvising under stress.

  • Job loss: immediate, 30-day, 90-day actions.
  • Market downturn: how to respond, not react.
  • Unexpected expenses: where the money comes from, by design.
