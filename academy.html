THE CURRICULUM.

Master the 12 Pillars of Sovereignty.

01

Iron Pot Millionaire

Iron Pot Millionaire is a ruthless, disciplined approach to building wealth by stripping life down to what actually matters. It’s not about being cheap — it’s about being surgical.

How to Run It

  • • Define non-negotiables: housing, food, essential tools.
  • • Cut lifestyle fluff for a defined season.
  • • Track progress weekly, not emotionally — numbers only.

Core Idea

"Define the leanest, most focused version of your life and run it long enough to radically change your financial trajectory."

02

Wealth Architecture

Wealth Architecture is about designing your financial life like a system, not a series of reactions. Every dollar has a job. Nothing is random.

How to Use It

  1. Map current accounts into the three layers.
  2. Identify gaps in defense or offense.
  3. Automate contributions so the system builds itself.

The 3 Layers

  • CASH: Reserves and operating capital.
  • DEFENSE: Insurance, legal, and risk buffers.
  • OFFENSE: Investments and asymmetric growth.
03

Scenario Planning

Scenario Planning gives you pre-built responses to financial shocks. When something hits, you don’t improvise — you execute.

How to Build It

  • • Define "Day 1, 30, and 90" responses for each scenario.
  • • Pre-decide what you will cut, sell, pause, or double down on.
  • • Review annually as your numbers change.

Key Scenarios

Job loss, Market downturns, Unexpected medical hits, and Legal exposure defense.

04

Debt Neutralization

Debt is a tax on the undisciplined. We use high-velocity repayment tactics to destroy interest carry-costs and reclaim your flow.

The Mission

Neutralize consumer liabilities to pivot capital directly into your Offense layer.

The Strategy

List debts by interest-to-impact ratio. Isolate target. Apply surgical pressure. Reclaim the energy.

05

Asset Allocation

Concentrated positions in anti-fragile assets. We ignore the mutual fund trap and focus on real estate, private debt, and hard assets.

The Core

Diversification is for the confused. Sovereignty requires concentration in what you can influence.

The Focus
