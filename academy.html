THE CURRICULUM.

12 Pillars. One Protocol.

01

Iron Pot Millionaire

Wealth begins by reclaiming your labor from lifestyle inflation. We strip life to the surgical essentials to create the capital surplus required for war.

How to Run It

  • • Define "Non-Negotiables": Shelter, Fuel, High-Protein Food, Essential Tools.
  • • Slash "The Fluff": Subscriptions, dining out, and status-signaling purchases.
  • • Commit to a 6-month "Iron Season" to build your first major reserve.

Core Idea

"Define the leanest version of your life and run it long enough to radically change your financial trajectory."

02

Wealth Architecture

Wealth is a plumbing problem. We build a three-reservoir system that automatically filters income into its proper destination.

Income Engine
Layer 01: Cash
Layer 02: Defense
Layer 03: Offense

How to Run It

  • • Establish separate accounts for Liquidity, The Moat, and The War-Chest.
  • • Set fixed percentage "Rules of Flow" for every dollar earned.
  • • Automate transfers to remove human emotion and hesitation.

Core Idea

"Wealth is not what you earn; it is the system that decides where your earnings go automatically."

03

Scenario Planning

Execute, don't improvise. We pre-decide our moves for every possible crisis so we can act while others panic.

EVENT: INCOME LOSS
DAY 01: KILL SUBS & FLUFF
DAY 30: LIQUIDATE OFFENSE
DAY 90: EMERGENCY EXIT

How to Run It

  • • Map "The Big 4": Job loss, Health hit, Market crash, Legal hit.
  • • Define Day 1, Day 30, and Day 90 survival protocols for each.
  • • Stress-test your current architecture against these scenarios monthly.

Core Idea

"Panic is a tax on the unprepared. Planning is the insurance policy for your sanity and your capital."

04

Debt Neutralization

Debt is a claim on your future energy. We use tactical destruction to reclaim your monthly flow and pivot it to offense.

How to Run It

  • • Target the "Impact Target" that frees the most monthly cash.
  • • Apply 100% of Iron Pot surplus to that single target until dead.
  • • "Roll" the payment into the next target (The Sniper Roll).

Core Idea

"Kill your liabilities before they kill your options. Reclaim your future labor today."

05

Sovereign Allocation

Stop buying the products they sell you. Move capital into concentrated positions in assets you can influence or control.

How to Run It

  • • Focus on "Hard Yield": Cash-flowing real estate and private debt.
  • • Cap public market exposure to 10% for pure "gambling" capital.
  • • Build a portfolio that yields regardless of stock market sentiment.

Core Idea

"Diversification is for people who don't know what they are doing. Sovereignty requires concentration."

06

Offensive Earning

The best defense is a high-powered offense. Scale your income engine to flood your architecture with volume.

How to Run It

  • • Identify and stack high-leverage skills (Sales, Systems, Leadership).
  • • Outsource all low-value tasks to maintain a high hourly rate.
  • • Focus on equity-based income engines over hours-for-dollars.

Core Idea

"Maximize the primary engine to outrun the erosion of inflation and taxation."

07

Tax Exposure Defense

Stop volunteering money to the state. Structural optimization to keep your labor's fruit.

How to Run It

  • • Audit your business entity (S-Corp, Trust, LLC) for leakage.
  • • Maximize depreciation through hard asset ownership.
  • • Plan exit strategies before taking entrance positions.

Core Idea

"Legal and moral tax defense is the highest-yielding investment you can make."
