THE CURRICULUM.

The 12-Pillar Framework

PILLAR 01

Iron Pot Millionaire

Iron Pot Millionaire is a ruthless, disciplined approach to building wealth by stripping life down to what actually matters. It’s not about being cheap — it’s about being surgical.

Core Idea

You define the leanest, most focused version of your life — your "iron pot" baseline — and run that version long enough to radically change your financial trajectory.

How to Run It

  • 01/ Define non-negotiables: housing, food, basic transport, essential tools.
  • 02/ Cut everything else: subscriptions, lifestyle fluff, status spending.
  • 03/ Stack cash aggressively and attack debt with intentional intensity.
  • 04/ Track progress weekly, not emotionally — numbers only.

When to Use It

"You're buried in debt and want out fast."
"You need to build an emergency fund now."

PILLAR 02

Wealth Architecture

Wealth Architecture is about designing your financial life like a system, not a series of reactions. Every dollar has a job. Every account has a purpose. Nothing is random.

Three-Layer Model

  • Cash layer: emergency fund, operating cash, short-term reserves.
  • Defense layer: insurance, buffers, legal structure, risk management.
  • Offense layer: investments, income expansion, asymmetric opportunities.

How to Use It

  1. Map your current accounts and assets into the three layers.
  2. Identify gaps: missing emergency fund, weak defense, or no offense.
  3. Set clear targets for each layer and automate contributions.

Soon: Visual Wealth Architecture diagram and PDF walkthrough.

PILLAR 03

Scenario Planning

Scenario Planning gives you pre-built responses to the financial shocks most people never prepare for. When something hits, you don’t improvise — you execute.

Key Scenarios

  • Job loss: income disappears, expenses stay.
  • Market downturn: assets drop, emotions spike.
  • Unexpected expense: health, car, family, or legal hits.

How to Build Your Playbooks

  1. Define what “day 1, day 30, day 90” looks like for each scenario.
  2. Pre-decide what you will cut, sell, pause, or double down on.
  3. Tie each scenario to your Wealth Architecture layers.
PILLAR 04

Debt Neutralization

Debt is a claim on your future energy. Neutralization is the tactical destruction of liabilities to reclaim your monthly cash flow.

Core Idea

Identify the "interest-to-impact" ratio of every liability and apply surgical pressure to kill the debt and pivot that energy into your Offense Layer.

How to Run It

  • / Isolate the "target" liability and apply all excess baseline capital.
  • / Use the "Sniper Method" to roll payments into the next target.
PILLAR 05

Opportunity War-Chest

Maintaining high liquidity is not "losing to inflation"—it is buying an option on future chaos. A War-Chest allows you to act when others are paralyzed.

Deployment Rules

  • 01/ Never touch the War-Chest for lifestyle needs.
  • 02/ Set "Strike Prices" for assets you want to own.
  • 03/ Deploy only when the math meets your pre-set criteria.
PILLAR 06

Offensive Earning

You cannot save your way to sovereignty if your income is stagnant. This is about maximizing the "Primary Engine" to increase capital volume.

How to Use It

  • / Audit your hourly value and outsource tasks.
  • / Identify high-leverage skills (Sales, Systems, Leadership).
  • / Reinvest into the engine until it hits peak velocity.
