THE CURRICULUM.

12 Pillars. One Mission.

01

Iron Pot Millionaire

Stop playing house. Strip life down to the surgical essentials to create a massive capital surplus. This is the foundation of all offensive maneuvers.

How to Run It

  • • Audit every cent: If it doesn't provide shelter, fuel, or growth, kill it.
  • • Define your "Baseline Number"—the absolute cost of survival.
  • • Direct 100% of the surplus into the Architecture (Pillar 02).

Core Idea

"You define the leanest, most focused version of your life—your 'iron pot' baseline—and run it long enough to radically change your trajectory."

02

Wealth Architecture

Wealth is a plumbing problem. We build a three-reservoir system that automatically filters income into its proper destination.

Income Engine
Layer 01: Cash
Layer 02: Defense
Layer 03: Offense

How to Run It

  • • Set up 3 specific accounts: Liquidity, The Moat, and The War-Chest.
  • • Automate fixed percentage flow from every paycheck.
  • • Never manually move money—let the plumbing work.

Core Idea

"Remove human emotion from the wealth-building process by building a system that doesn't require your permission to save."

03

Scenario Planning

Execute, don't improvise. We pre-decide our moves for every possible crisis so we can act while others panic.

EVENT: INCOME LOSS
DAY 01: KILL SUBS & FLUFF
DAY 30: LIQUIDATE OFFENSE
DAY 90: EXECUTE EMERGENCY EXIT

How to Run It

  • • Identify "The Big 4": Job loss, Health hit, Market crash, Legal hit.
  • • Write down Day 1, 30, and 90 actions for each.
  • • Review the playbook annually to adjust for your current scale.

Core Idea

"Panic is a tax on the unprepared. Planning is the insurance policy for your sanity and your capital."

04

Debt Neutralization

Debt is a claim on your future energy. We use tactical destruction to reclaim your monthly flow and pivot it to offense.

How to Run It

  • • Identify the "Impact Target": The debt that frees the most monthly cash.
  • • Apply 100% of "Iron Pot" surplus to that single target until dead.
  • • "Roll" the payment into the next target until the perimeter is clear.

Core Idea

"Kill your liabilities before they kill your options. Reclaim your future labor today."

05

Sovereign Allocation

Stop buying the products they sell you. Move capital into concentrated positions in assets you can influence.

How to Run It

  • • Limit public market exposure to automated index plays.
  • • Focus on "Hard Yield": Cash-flowing real estate or private debt.
  • • Build a portfolio that produces regardless of stock market sentiment.

Core Idea

"Diversification is for people who don't know what they are doing. Sovereignty requires concentration."

06

Offensive Earning

The best defense is a high-powered offense. Scale your income engine to flood your architecture with volume.

How to Run It

  • • Stack high-leverage skills (Sales, Tech, Content).
  • • Outsource low-value tasks to maintain a high hourly rate.
  • • Focus on equity-based income, not just hours-for-dollars.

Core Idea

"Maximize the primary engine to outrun the erosion of inflation and taxation."
