The Iron Pot Baseline

Iron Pot Millionaire is a surgical approach to building wealth. It’s about defining your leanest, most focused version of life—your "iron pot" baseline—and running it long enough to radically change your trajectory.

How to Run the Playbook:

  1. Define non-negotiables: Housing, clean food, basic transport, essential tools.
  2. Cut the fluff: No subscriptions, no status spending, no lifestyle creep.
  3. Stack cash: Attack debt or build assets with relentless intensity.
  4. Track progress: Weekly audits. Numbers don't have feelings.
